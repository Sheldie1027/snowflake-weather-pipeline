OVERVIEW: A heatwave is affecting major cities in India, with temperatures soaring above average levels. The high humidity in some areas has led to increased instances of rain and thunderstorms.

HOTTEST CITY: Delhi at 38.7°C
COOLEST CITY: Bengaluru at 19.7°C
KEY PATTERN: A high-pressure system is dominating the region, leading to clear skies and hot temperatures during the day, followed by thunderstorms and rain in the evening.
RECOMMENDATION: Residents are advised to stay hydrated and take necessary precautions to avoid heat-related illnesses, especially during the peak sun hours.
